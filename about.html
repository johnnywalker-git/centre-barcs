About

Get to know me...

Hi I’m Ella, I’m 23 years old and have an unconditional love for all animals big and small! As pictured above I have my own horse, Dante, and a Cockerpoo, Rue, these two trouble makers are my world and the reason I do what I do.

I’ve always known I wanted a job surrounding animals which is what lead me to study at a top agricultural college.

On top of the knowledge gained from this I have spent hours self researching and learning about all things dogs behaviour and have been fortunate enough to open this business.

What do CentreBarks provide?

We want to stand out from the crowd with controlled and monitored walks. This doesn’t mean we’re taking the fun out of a good play! It just means we have the knowledge to know when some dogs have had enough and need to dial in their focus and build their self control to make walks more enjoyable for all.


We do this by switching up our walks and the means in which we play and interact with the group. We also love a sniffy walk and as always plenty of training and engagement with the walker thrown in.

Must. Sniff. Everything.

We love sniffy walks here at Centrebarks and as many experts will agree a 20 minute sniff is more enriching for a dog than an hour walk! Combine this with a treasure trove of different toys and friends to play with, your pooch will definitely be entertained and tired out after our play time!
