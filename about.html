All about us...

What do we provide?

Being one of the only dog walkers with a fully enclosed private field in the neighbourhood means we can provide you peace of mind that your dog is statistically safer and at less risk than with other providers. We pride ourselves on being able to cater for most dogs* and allow a place for off lead play and a good non interrupted sniff!

Funny line about sniffing things

We love sniffy walks here at Centrebarks and as many experts will agree a 20 minute sniff is more enriching for a dog than an hour walk! Combine this with a treasure trove of different toys and friends to play with, your pooch will definitely be entertained and tired out after our play time!

Social dogs

Showing off our clients is something we love doing, with our pup of the week competitions and great knowledge of social media you’ll be able to keep up to date with your dog whilst they’re out and about with their friends!
